About Campfire Pune

Campfire Pune is an exciting game jam event where developers, designers, and creative minds come together to build innovative games in just 12 hours. Hosted at Newton School of Technology, this event brings together the passion of the local game development community.

Whether you're an experienced developer or just starting out, Campfire Pune welcomes everyone. Our mission is to foster creativity, collaboration, and learning in a fun and supportive environment.

Join us for a day of coding, designing, and creating amazing games that you can share with the world!

🎁 What we offered

  • 🕹️ Hands‑on GameMaker Studio workshop
  • 🧠 Friendly mentors & peer feedback
  • 🗳️ Peer voting to pick standout projects
  • 🎁 Prizes: Logitech gaming mice, free .xyz domains, Craftpix subs, stickers, tees, chocolates, meals & fun!
